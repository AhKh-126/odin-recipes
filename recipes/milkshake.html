Vanilla Milkshake recipe

Description

A vanilla milkshake recipe that's very easy to make. Add chocolate, berries, peaches, or whatever you like.

Ingredients

This recipe is for 2 servings

  • 2 cups vanilla ice cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Blend ice cream, milk, and vanilla extract together in a blender until smooth.
  3. Pour into glasses and serve.
